What is Summerween?

Summerween is the celebration of all things scary and spooky- but in the summer. Driven by our mutual love of Halloween, we wanted to create an event which would allow us to celebrate our favorite holiday a second time! And also wear great costumes without freezing our asses off.


The name is inspired by Gravity Falls, the concept by various Asian festivals (Bon), and the aesthetic (purple and black, moths for bats, watermelon for pumpkins) all our own, we bring to you SUMMERWEEN!


TLDR: Come to our summer Halloween party! Wear a costume if you want, there will be food & booze
